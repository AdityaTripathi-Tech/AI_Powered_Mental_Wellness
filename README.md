##  Overview

*Mentality* is an *AI-driven Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) platform* designed to *detect and heal mental health disorders* such as *OCD, PTSD, phobias, eating disorders, and borderline disorder* ‚Äî conditions that often lack direct medical cures.

It leverages *Artificial Intelligence, **Blockchain, and **Gamified Digital Therapy* to make mental healthcare *personalized, secure, and accessible* for everyone.

---

##  Problem Statement

> *AI-Enabled Early Disease Detection (Humans & Animals)* ‚Äì Focusing on the early detection and treatment of mental disorders.

Mental health conditions are often diagnosed late due to stigma, lack of awareness, or limited access to professionals.  
*Mentality* bridges this gap by offering *early AI-based mental health analysis, interactive therapy, and **real-time support* through a secure digital platform.

##  System Architecture

1. *User Input:* Mood updates, journal entries, webcam emotion tracking  
2. *AI Engine:* Processes emotion + behavior data ‚Üí generates personalized CBT  
3. *Therapist Layer:* Recommends suitable therapists and resources  
4. *Blockchain Layer:* Encrypts and stores data for security and transparency  
5. *Feedback Loop:* AI learns continuously and refines therapy recommendations
